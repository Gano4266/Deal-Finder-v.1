# Project Brief

## Goal

Build a lean MVP that helps people in Wilmington, NC find real local restaurant food deals for tonight: taco nights, wing deals, burger specials, BOGO meals, happy hour food, kids-eat-free nights, and similar source-backed specials.

## Audience

- Local diners looking for affordable dinner or happy hour food.
- Visitors already in Wilmington who want an easy, trustworthy list.
- Restaurant operators who want current specials represented accurately.
- Internal reviewers responsible for validating and refreshing deal data.

## Promise

Deal Finder should show fewer deals if needed, but every public deal must be traceable to evidence. The product earns trust by displaying source links, freshness, restrictions, and uncertainty instead of pretending messy information is clean.

## Constraints

- Wilmington, North Carolina only for the first MVP.
- Nearby markets are excluded unless explicitly approved later.
- AI can extract and normalize information, but AI output is never a source.
- Social media is valuable but volatile, so it starts as a manual or browser-assisted review source.
- The first user-facing app path is mobile-first web/PWA, not Xcode.

## Non-Goals

- National expansion.
- Automated publication without review.
- Fully automated Instagram/Facebook scraping.
- Restaurant self-serve dashboards.
- Native iOS or Android builds.
- Personalized recommendations or loyalty programs.

## Success Metrics

- 25 Wilmington restaurants seeded with at least one source each.
- At least 10 trustworthy published deals during the first operating pass.
- 100% of public deals have source evidence and freshness metadata.
- Stale or ambiguous deals route to review instead of staying public.
- One reviewer can maintain the first 25 restaurants in 3-5 hours per week.
