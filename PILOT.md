# Friend Pilot

## Who To Invite

Start with 5 to 10 Wilmington-area friends who will actually try a food deal, not just click around.

## Invite Message

```text
I’m testing a tiny Wilmington restaurant deal app.

It shows source-backed food specials for tonight, with links/proof for each deal. It’s still a static prototype, so please confirm with the restaurant/source before ordering.

Try it here:
PASTE_LIVE_URL

On iPhone: open in Safari, tap Share, then Add to Home Screen.

If anything looks wrong or stale, tap “Report issue” on the deal page.
```

## What To Ask

- Did `/tonight` answer what you wanted quickly?
- Were any deals obviously wrong, stale, or confusing?
- Was the source/proof visible enough to trust?
- Was the app too crowded on your phone?
- Did Add to Home Screen work?
- Did Report issue open a usable email draft?

## Pilot Rules

- Keep the group small until the first recheck cycle is complete.
- Treat every report as a review task.
- Do not add unsourced tips as public deals.
- Keep admin routes disabled in production.
