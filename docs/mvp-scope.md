# MVP Scope

## Included

- Wilmington, NC restaurant food deals and specials.
- Tonight-oriented browsing.
- Restaurant-owned websites, menus, official social posts, official ordering pages, newsletters, direct confirmations, and reviewed secondary sources.
- Manual or browser-assisted review for volatile sources.
- Source links, evidence dates, confidence status, restrictions, and freshness metadata.
- Admin review before publication.

## Excluded By Default

- Wilmington, Delaware.
- Leland, Wrightsville Beach, Carolina Beach, Kure Beach, Hampstead, Southport, and other nearby areas.
- Alcohol-only deal discovery.
- Fully automated social scraping.
- User accounts.
- Native iOS or Android apps.
- Restaurant self-serve portals.
- Paid placements, affiliate links, coupons, contests, or loyalty programs.

Nearby areas can be added later only through an explicit scope decision in `DECISIONS.md`.

## MVP Milestone

The first operating milestone is not "all Wilmington deals." It is:

- 25 Wilmington restaurants seeded.
- At least one source per restaurant.
- Source-backed deals reviewed and published.
- Stale and ambiguous deals routed to review.
- A visible deal can be traced back to evidence.
